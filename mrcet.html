What is MRCET?

MRCET is a technical institute in Hyderabad, India. It was established in 2004 and is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University. The college offers a variety of engineering programs, including electronics and electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and computer science and engineering.

The college has a number of departments, including the Department of Information Technology, the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, and the Department of Humanities and Sciences. The college also offers several programs, including the Internet of Things (IoT), Data Science, and Mobile Application Development.

  • MRCET has a well-balanced mix of experienced and energetic faculty members.
  • MRCET has a number of well-equipped laboratories.
  • MRCET has a spacious seminar hall.
  • MRCET has a Centre of Excellence in Big Data Analytics.
  • MRCET has a wheelchair accessible entrance and parking lot.
